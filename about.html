NWSL

The National Women Soccer League (NWSL) is the prominent soccer league here in the US. It consists of 12 teams spread across the US. Two of which reside is California, Angel City FC in Los Angeles and Wave FC in San Diego. It was founded in 2013 so it isn't the oldest women's league but is the most accessible to those in the US to go to games and watch them on Paramount+, Twitch and the occasional CBS sports broadcast.

The A League woman is the prodominent league in Australia. It consists of 11 teams and was founded in 2008. This one is less accessible to many in the US due to the time difference and the restictions on the streaming services used to broadcast the games.

Fifa is the all incompassing governing body of all things soccer. Both men and women leagues follow the guidelines given by Fifa. They also are the ones who decide where and what time of year World Cups will be held.
