Women's soccer is a growing sport, there are many leagues around the world yet people tend to ignore them.

There have only been 8 Women's World Cups compared to the 21 of the Men's World Cups. The very first Women's world cup was held in China in the year 1991. Women's national teams tend to gain more attention and are more broadly televised. The leagues however are a lot more difficult to access when it comes to watching the games.

The Women's World Cup is always a year after the Men's World Cup. Meaning that there will be a Women's World Cup next year that will be show casinf talented players from leagues around the world. Click the trophy under Fifa to learn more.
